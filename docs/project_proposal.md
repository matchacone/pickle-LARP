## Client Background
This platform serves a dynamic, dual-sided market consisting of pickleball enthusiasts searching for places to play and court owners looking to streamline their booking processes. Pickleball is currently experiencing massive growth, making it one of the trendiest sports today. However, this surge in popularity has created a significant bottleneck. Players are actively hunting for available courts, but facilities are frequently operating at maximum capacity, often leaving enthusiasts frustrated because the courts are "halos puno2" (almost always full). Currently, players must rely on inefficient methods, like calling multiple facilities individually or checking disparate social media pages, just to find an open slot. 

## Statement of Need 
There is a distinct lack of a unified, centralized platform that bridges the gap between these scattered pickleball facilities and eager players. To resolve this, our application will provide a comprehensive solution that brings all local courts and customers together in one place. By centralizing the scheduling process, customers will be able to easily discover facilities near them and seamlessly reserve specific time slots depending on real-time court availability. This not only eliminates the frustration of manual searching for players but also brings much-needed operational efficiency and visibility to facility owners. 

## The Software as Response to the Need 
To effectively meet these needs, the proposed software will be a highly accessible web application designed to eliminate booking friction without requiring users to immediately download a native app. At its core, the platform acts as an informational and location-aware hub where registered users can instantly view court availability based on their geographic location and preferred playing time. Furthermore, the application features a robust scheduling engine designed to organize various events, matches, and casual play. This ensures seamless scheduling with little to no conflicts or double-booking issues for either the users or the facility managers. 

## The Software’s Main Functionalities
To deliver a complete end-to-end experience, the platform will rely on several core functionalities:

* **Real-time Reservation System:** Allows users to effortlessly browse calendars and book open time slots.
* **Integrated Payment System:** Securely handles court rental fees upfront directly within the app.
* **Facility Management Admin Dashboard:** Gives court owners the tools they need to oversee their daily availability, track revenue, and manage customer bookings.
* **Detailed Court Profiles:** Provides players with essential upfront information such as parking availability, indoor versus outdoor designations, and the option to add rental equipment like paddles to their booking.

# Project Requirements Document: Pick All

## 1. Executive Summary
**Pick All** is a high-fidelity booking platform for racquet sports (specifically Pickleball and Padel), inspired by the seamless user experience of Fresha.com. The platform connects facility owners with players, offering a streamlined discovery and booking engine.

## 2. Brand Identity: Kinetic Court
The project utilizes the **Kinetic Court** design system, characterized by:
*   **Aesthetic:** High-energy, athletic, and modern.
*   **Primary Palette:** #d1fe00 (Neon Court Green) against high-contrast 'Asphalt' (#1a1c1d) and clean 'Mist' (#f3f4f5) surfaces.
*   **Typography:** *Plus Jakarta Sans* for a clean, technical, yet approachable feel.
*   **Shape:** 8px (Round Eight) corner radius for a balanced, professional look.

## 3. User Personas
*   **The Player:** Seeking quick discovery of local courts, transparent pricing, and instant booking.
*   **The Facility Owner:** Focused on maximizing court occupancy, managing rentals (paddles/balls), and building a local community.

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Discovery & Search
*   **Geospatial Search:** Ability to find courts by location.
*   **Availability Filtering:** Real-time filtering by Date, Time, and Player Count.
*   **Quick Filters:** "Indoor," "Outdoor," "Pro Shop," and "Top Rated" pills for rapid refinement.
*   **View Options:** Toggle between interactive map views and optimized grid/card layouts.

### 4.2 Court Details
*   **Media Gallery:** High-resolution imagery of facility surfaces and amenities.
*   **Service Catalog:** Transparent pricing for court hire and equipment rentals (e.g., Pro Carbon Paddles).
*   **Social Proof:** Integrated review system and facility ratings.
*   **Conversion Sidebar:** A sticky booking widget for immediate scheduling.

### 4.3 Booking & Checkout
*   **Low-Friction Flow:** Integrated payment support (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Credit Card).
*   **Schedule Verification:** Clear summary of Date and Time windows prior to commitment.
*   **Cancellation Policy:** Transparent 24-hour refund window to build user trust.

### 4.4 Onboarding
*   **Kinetic Sign-up:** High-energy join flow to encourage community participation.
*   **Secure Authentication:** Standardized login/signup protocols.

## 5. Technical Specifications
*   **Frontend:** Responsive HTML5/Tailwind CSS.
*   **Device Support:** Desktop-first with mobile-responsive navigation (hamburger menus for secondary links).
*   **Accessibility:** High-contrast color ratios and semantic HTML structure.

## 6. Project Roadmap
*   **Phase 1 (Completed):** Design System, Home Discovery, Search Results, Login/Signup, Court Details, and Checkout Flow.
*   **Phase 2 (Proposed):** User Dashboard (Booking Management), Booking Success/Confirmation screens, and Facility Owner "Partner" portal.
